SEVA: Your Home Service Solution

SEVA is a comprehensive web-based application offering a wide range of home services such as AC servicing, refrigerator repair, water pump maintenance, and more. This innovative project was developed by our team consisting of Haikal Hamizan Hazmi, Muhammad Edlyn, and Muhammad Rizky Darmawan.

SEVA aims to streamline the process of booking and managing home services through a user-friendly interface. Whether you need urgent repairs or routine maintenance, SEVA connects you with qualified service providers efficiently and effectively.



GovSpeak

GovSpeak leverages web technology to create a user-friendly interface where citizens can access government information effortlessly. Through this platform, citizens can monitor government activities, assess performance, and contribute to policy-making processes. By promoting transparency, accountability, and public participation, GovSpeak aims to build a stronger and more responsive government that meets the needs and expectations of its citizens.
